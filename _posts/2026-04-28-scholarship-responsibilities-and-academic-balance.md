### Scholarship Responsibilities and Time Management

After the completion of midterm exams, our regular university classes resumed on 15th April. As academic activities returned to their normal routine, another important and unexpected responsibility appeared in my life only a few days later. On 20th April, I received the exciting news that I had been selected for the CM Honours Scholarship.

It was a proud and motivating moment for me because receiving a scholarship felt like recognition for my hard work and academic consistency. However, along with happiness came pressure and responsibility because the deadline for document submission was extremely close. We were required to submit all documents before 22nd April, which gave me very limited time to complete the entire process.

The scholarship documentation was not simple because, in addition to basic educational and personal documents, I also had to submit a deed of agreement that required official signatures and verification stamps. Since these formalities could not be completed easily at the university, I initially planned to travel back home to manage the process personally.

At the same time, another challenge existed because I had a Database Systems lab scheduled for the next day. The lab timing was expected to continue until 4:00 PM, while my train departure time was 4:30 PM. Missing the train could create major travel problems and delay the scholarship work even further.

Because of the urgency of the situation, I discussed everything with my Database teacher and explained the scholarship deadline along with my travel timing. Thankfully, the teacher understood my situation and cooperated with me. After checking my work and confirming my train schedule, he evaluated my marks and allowed me to leave early so I could manage the remaining responsibilities on time.

The Database lab itself was also an important learning experience. Our task was based on normalization, where we had to convert unnormalized data into First Normal Form (1NF) and then transform it into Second Normal Form (2NF). After completing the normalization process, we implemented the solution using MySQL Workbench. This practical activity helped us understand how databases are organized properly to reduce redundancy and improve efficiency.

Before finalizing my travel plan, I discussed my scholarship situation with one of my former school teachers. I explained that I urgently needed signatures and stamps from authorized officers for the required documents. He kindly offered his support and told me to send the documents to his home so he could help arrange everything.

After hearing this, I contacted my father and requested him to print the documents on stamp paper and deliver them to my teacher. Because of their cooperation and support, I eventually cancelled my travel plan entirely. This decision saved both time and energy while teaching me an important lesson about communication, trust, and the value of supportive people during difficult situations.

### Communication Skills Activities and Confidence Building

After the weekend ended, we attended our Communication Skills lab on Monday where the teacher organized a pair-based talk show activity. In this task, one student had to perform as a host while the other acted as a guest discussing a selected topic.

Me and my friend Tahir decided to choose the topic:
**“Role of Social Media in Student Life.”**

During the activity, Tahir performed as the host while I acted as the guest. We discussed both positive and negative aspects of social media, its influence on students, and how it affects communication, learning habits, and daily routines. We also included practical examples from student life to make the discussion more realistic and engaging.

At first, standing and speaking in front of classmates created some nervousness, but once the discussion started, our confidence improved gradually. At the end of the presentation, both students and the teacher asked us several questions related to the topic, which we answered successfully.

Alhamdulillah, we received **4 out of 5 marks** in the activity. More importantly, the experience improved my speaking confidence, communication style, and ability to respond to questions publicly.

After this activity, our teacher assigned another continuous communication task that would continue until final examinations. According to the instructions, every student had to record a 10-minute English-speaking video daily. Students could choose either a vlog format or any random informative topic while speaking directly in English.

Initially, the task seemed difficult because recording long videos every day required confidence, fluency, and consistency. However, I also realized that this activity could significantly improve speaking ability and communication confidence over time through regular practice.

### Unexpected Quizzes and Learning Outcomes

The very next day after our Communication Skills activity, we faced another academic challenge in the form of two surprise quizzes from Calculus and Statistics.

Many students became worried because the quizzes were announced suddenly, leaving very little preparation time. The Calculus quiz turned out to be particularly difficult for most students. Several students scored below average marks, while some students even received zero.

After observing the overall class performance, the teacher decided to cancel the quiz entirely and announced that it would be conducted again after two days. This decision reduced pressure on students and provided another opportunity for preparation.

On the other hand, the Statistics quiz went comparatively better because the questions were more example-based and easier to attempt logically. Students felt more comfortable solving practical examples compared to the difficult Calculus questions.

This phase of university life became a combination of responsibilities, academic pressure, communication activities, and quick decision-making. Managing scholarship deadlines alongside university tasks taught me how important time management and planning are during busy academic periods.

At the same time, communication activities helped improve confidence and speaking skills, while quizzes and practical labs continued strengthening academic understanding. Every challenge during this period contributed not only to my educational growth but also to my personal development and ability to handle responsibilities independently.

Overall, this experience taught me the importance of adaptability, consistency, communication, and balancing multiple responsibilities together while continuing to move forward confidently in university life.
